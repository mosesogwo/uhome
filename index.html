About Us

Unekwuojo Home of Mercy and Emancipation Ministry was founded out of deep compassion and a divine mandate to respond to the growing crisis of abandoned children and widows affected by insurgency, terrorism, and religious persecution. What began as a humble initiative has grown into a structured ministry impacting hundreds of lives through shelter, education, and empowerment.

Our Vision

A compassionate society where no vulnerable soul is left behind

Our Mission

Providing holistic care and restoration through love, faith, and practical support

Our Programs

Our work is grounded in Christian doctrine, inspired by Christ's command to care for the least in society. We uphold biblical values of mercy, justice, love, and selfless service.

Orphan Care & Residential Home

We provide a secure, nurturing environment where orphaned and abandoned children receive shelter, food, healthcare, education, and spiritual guidance.

Widow Support Programme

Support for widows through financial assistance, counselling, skills training, and empowerment initiatives.

Relief for Victims of Insurgency

Emergency intervention, relief materials, and rehabilitation for families affected by violent conflicts.

Education Sponsorship

School enrollment, uniforms, books, and academic mentoring for vulnerable children.

Vocational Training & Empowerment

Skill acquisition programmes including tailoring, farming, ICT, and small business management.

Trauma Healing & Counselling

Professional and spiritual support for emotional restoration and reintegration.
