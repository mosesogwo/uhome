About Us

Unekwuojo Home of Mercy and Emancipation Ministry was founded out of deep compassion and a divine mandate to respond to the growing crisis of abandoned children and widows affected by insurgency, terrorism, and religious persecution. What began as a humble initiative has grown into a structured ministry impacting hundreds of lives through shelter, education, and empowerment.

Our Programs

Our work is grounded in Christian doctrine, inspired by Christ's command to care for the least in society. We uphold biblical values of mercy, justice, love, and selfless service.

Orphan Care & Residential Home

We provide a secure, nurturing environment where orphaned and abandoned children receive shelter, food, healthcare, education, and spiritual guidance.

Widow Support Programme

Support for widows through financial assistance, counselling, skills training, and empowerment initiatives.

Relief for Victims of Insurgency

Emergency intervention, relief materials, and rehabilitation for families affected by violent conflicts.

Education Sponsorship

School enrollment, uniforms, books, and academic mentoring for vulnerable children.

Vocational Training & Empowerment

Skill acquisition programmes including tailoring, farming, ICT, and small business management.

Trauma Healing & Counselling

Professional and spiritual support for emotional restoration and reintegration.

2026 Prayer Newsletter
Introduction

Unekwuojo Home of Mercy and Emancipation Ministry is a Christian charitable ministry committed to caring for widows, orphans and vulnerable children, particularly children of deceased pastors and victims of Fulani herdsmen attacks. Some of these children live with us in our home, while many others are supported through educational sponsorships and welfare care.

We humbly invite you to stand with us in prayer as we trust God for His mercy, provision, protection, and salvation in the year 2026.

A. Prayer Focus for Unekwuojo HOME Ministry
  1. Orphans and Vulnerable Children
    Pray for all the orphans and vulnerable children under the care of the ministry—those residing with us and those benefiting from our external educational sponsorships. Pray for their physical well-being, emotional stability, spiritual growth, and academic success.
  2. Trauma Healing and Protection
    Pray for divine healing for children and widows who are survivors of traumatic Fulani herdsmen attacks. Ask the Lord to heal their emotional wounds, restore joy, and grant His continual protection over their lives.
  3. Provision for Education and Upkeep
    Pray for God’s provision for the education and upkeep of the children and ministry team. Educational sponsorship for each child ranges from ₦30,000 to ₦100,000 per annum. Presently, over 50 children are waiting to benefit from this sponsorship.
  4. Permanent Office and Accommodation
    Pray concerning our need for a permanent office and accommodation for the ministry. A property adjacent to our residence—consisting of sufficient land and two buildings with twin two-bedroom flats (eight bedrooms in total)—has been offered for sale at ₦10 million. Pray that if it pleases the Lord to give us this property, He will graciously provide the required funds.
  5. 2026 Children Holiday Camp
    Pray for the success of the 2nd Edition of the Children Holiday Camp for Orphans, scheduled to hold from 2nd–5th September 2026. This year, we plan to present each participant with an English/Igala Audio Bible. The total estimated cost of the camp is ₦3,050,000.
  6. Expansion of Support for Vulnerable Children
    Pray that the Lord will raise men and women with compassionate hearts and a burden to care for orphans and vulnerable children. There are countless children around us in desperate need of help.
  7. Salvation and Spiritual Growth of Our Children
    Pray earnestly that all the children under our care will come to know the Lord personally, grow in faith, and walk in obedience to Christ.
  8. God’s Blessing Upon the Ministry
    Pray for God’s abundant blessings upon Unekwuojo HOME Ministry—spiritually, materially, and administratively—as we serve Him faithfully.
